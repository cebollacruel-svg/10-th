COLEGIO NOCTURNO DE CARIARI
Listening Exam – First Semester 2026 · 10th Graders

Teacher: Saiden Ortiz Gómez  |  Total Points: 18 (10%)  |  Allotted Time: one lesson

Instructions: This exam has 6 audios. You will hear each audio and answer 3 questions about it. Each audio can be played up to 4 times only. Choose the correct answer for each question.

Multiple Choice — 18 points

Instructions: You will hear a person talking about their job. Choose the correct answer for each question.

Remaining plays: 4

1. What is the speaker's job?

2. What time does the speaker usually arrive home?

3. Why does the speaker often stay later than 5:00 PM?

Instructions: You will hear two friends talking about future jobs and their families. Choose the correct answer for each question.

Remaining plays: 4

4. What does Charlie's mother do?

6. What is special about Charlie's father's photos?

Remaining plays: 4

8. How many classes does Tom have every day?

Instructions: You will hear two friends planning to go to the cinema. Choose the correct answer for each question.

Remaining plays: 4

12. What time will they meet at the cinema?

Instructions: You will hear a phone conversation between two friends. Choose the correct answer for each question.
